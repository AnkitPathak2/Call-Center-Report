# 📞 Call Center Report 2023 - Excel Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The Call Center Report 2023 Dashboard is an interactive Excel-based business intelligence solution designed to analyze call center operations, customer satisfaction, revenue generation, and representative performance.

This dashboard transforms raw call center data into meaningful insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance and make informed decisions.



## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Monitor call center performance.
- Track monthly call trends.
- Analyze customer satisfaction ratings.
- Compare representative productivity.
- Evaluate revenue generated from customer interactions.
- Identify operational improvement opportunities.



## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization



## 📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Calls - 1,000 
Total Revenue - $96,623 
Total Call Duration - 89,850 
Average Rating - 3.9 
Happy Customers - 307 


## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 📞 Call Trend Analysis
Visualizes monthly call volume trends to identify peak and low-performing periods.

### 💰 Revenue Analysis
Compares revenue generated by different customer service representatives.

### 👥 Gender Distribution
Analyzes male and female caller distribution across cities.

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction Analysis
Displays rating distribution to evaluate customer experience.

### 🏙️ City-wise Analysis
Tracks customer activity from:
- Cincinnati
- Cleveland
- Columbus

### 🧑‍💼 Representative Performance
Evaluates performance metrics for:
- R01
- R02
- R03
- R04
- R05

### 📋 Detailed Performance Matrix
Provides customer-level revenue contribution and representative-wise performance breakdown.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue exceeded **$96K** across 1,000 customer calls.
- Customer ratings are concentrated around **4 and 5**, indicating positive satisfaction levels.
- Call volume peaked during specific months, highlighting seasonal demand patterns.
- Representative performance varies significantly across revenue and call volume metrics.
- Customer demographics differ across cities, providing opportunities for targeted service strategies.
